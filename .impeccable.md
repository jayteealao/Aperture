## Design Context

### Users
Solo developers and power users self-hosting AI coding agents (Claude Code, Codex, Gemini) on a VPS. They access sessions remotely from browsers or mobile, often managing 5-10+ concurrent AI agents across different repos. They value fast switching, credential isolation, and being in control of their infrastructure. Context: at a desk during focused coding sessions, sometimes checking in from mobile.

### Brand Personality
**Capable, polished, fast.** Aperture should feel like a tool built by someone who uses it daily — opinionated in the right ways, never getting in the way. It respects the user's intelligence without being cold or clinical. Think Linear's polish with Raycast's keyboard-first efficiency.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone**: Polished consumer app — smooth, intentional, refined. Not a dashboard, not a chat wrapper, not a showcase.
- **Theme**: Dark-first (developers in focused sessions), with a functional light mode.
- **Current palette**: Warm amber primary (oklch 0.75/0.14/55 dark, 0.52/0.12/55 light), muted slate-blue accent (oklch 0.62/0.10/250), warm-tinted neutrals on near-black backgrounds (hue 55).
- **Anti-references**: Generic SaaS dashboards, ChatGPT/Claude.ai chat-only UIs, over-designed glassmorphism showcases, terminal/CLI-only brutalism.
- **References**: Linear (polish, speed, keyboard-first), Raycast (density, snappiness), Vercel dashboard (clean, developer-focused).

### Design Principles
1. **Speed over spectacle** — Every interaction should feel instant. Animation serves function, not decoration.
2. **Density without clutter** — Power users want information, not whitespace. But every element must earn its place.
3. **Keyboard-first, mouse-friendly** — Primary flows work without a mouse. Visual affordances still exist for discoverability.
4. **Distinctive but not distracting** — The UI should have personality without becoming the focus. The user's AI sessions are the star.
5. **Honest feedback** — System status, errors, and loading states are always visible and clear. No silent failures.
